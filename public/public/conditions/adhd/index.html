ADHD Care — Cincinnati & NKY

ADHD can make school, work, and home feel messy. We test carefully and build a plan that helps you focus without losing yourself.

Signs & Symptoms

  • Trouble with focus, sleep, or mood
  • Stress at school, work, or home
  • Feeling stuck or overwhelmed

How We Help

  • Clear assessment and a plan you understand
  • Safe medication options when useful
  • Skills for sleep, routines, and calm
